About NovaStay

NovaStay is India's leading luxury villa rental platform, offering handpicked villas across the most scenic destinations. Founded in 2015, we have hosted over 2 lakh guests and built a network of homeowners who trust us to manage their properties.

Our mission is to make vacation rentals effortless and memorable. Every villa in our collection is personally verified for quality, hygiene, and comfort. We provide 24/7 caretaker support, in-house chefs, and curated local experiences to ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.
